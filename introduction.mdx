Our tax engine API is a powerful tool that automates the calculation and reporting of taxes for businesses and developers. It integrates seamlessly with various financial and e-commerce platforms, ensuring accurate tax computation based on current regulations and jurisdictions. By utilizing NumeralHQ's tax engine API, organizations can streamline their tax processes, reduce errors, and ensure compliance with ever-changing tax laws.

## What you will need

**Download the SDK**

To integrate with our tax engine API, users can choose from three available SDKs, each designed to cater to different programming languages and development environments. This flexibility ensures seamless integration and ease of use regardless of the user's preferred technology stack.

## Getting Started

To get started, first create a Numeral account to access the tax engine API. Next, generate test API keys from your dashboard to begin integrating and testing the API functionalities. Finally, upload your first product to the platform to see how the tax engine calculates and applies the relevant taxes.
